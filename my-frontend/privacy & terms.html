Privacy Policy

Our Philosophy: Your Data is Your Own

This application was built from the ground up with a privacy-first principle. Your creations, characters, and conversations belong exclusively to you — and only you.

Your Data — Always Local

All data you generate in this app — including characters, chat histories, personas, images, and settings — is stored exclusively on your own device within your browser's local storage (IndexedDB). There is no cloud storage and no account system.

The developer has no access to your stored data and never will. Nothing leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself.

API Requests

By default, when you send a message, your browser connects directly to your chosen AI provider (e.g., OpenRouter) using the API key you provide. Your key and chat content are sent directly to that third-party service — they never pass through any server operated by this project.

Please review the privacy policy of your chosen AI provider for details on how they handle your data on their end.

No Censorship, No Filtering

Because all content stays local and no central infrastructure is involved, there is no censorship or content filtering applied by this application. You have complete freedom to create the characters and stories you want. What happens in your browser, stays in your browser.

Your Responsibility

Because your data exists only on your device, you are solely responsible for keeping it safe. Please use the "Export Data" feature regularly to create backups. Clearing your browser data will erase everything.
